From the Founder

Called a med spa to book an appointment. No answer. Called again later. Same thing. So I booked somewhere else in 30 seconds and moved on.

That clinic likely never knew they lost me. No alert. No follow-up. Just missed revenue walking out the door.

It made me think — how many bookings disappear like that every week?

So I built Vizzop. An AI receptionist that answers every call, books directly into your calendar, and handles reschedules and cancellations. 24/7. No missed calls. No voicemail gaps. No lost bookings. Building from New Zealand, solving a problem I kept running into.
